About us

Who we are

Plan B Wealth was established in the year 2018 with the intention of providing ethical and honest services to Clients in the areas of personal finance and investment planning. With growing affluence in India, a need was felt for personalized services in these areas & hence Plan B Wealth was brought into existence to address this. With a unique ‘Client First’ Vision, Plan B Wealth strives to offer the best products and services customized to meet the financial objectives of Clients. The Mission of Plan B Wealth is to create wealthy Clients through financial education and active service. We believe that our success is firmly tied to ‘Client Delight’ and we will leave no stones unturned to achieve this. With rich experience in the field of financial and investment planning, our team is best placed to achieve our vision of ‘Client First’ and mission of ‘Client Delight’.
